AMC profile

Automated Motor Control has been in existence since 1978. The company was originally registered as a Pty Ltd but changed to a closed corporation in July 2004.

AMC is a privately owned company under director R. Du Plooy.


Our core business is the design, manufacture and installation of electrical systems for the HVAC industry.


Other fields covered are motor control centres for industrial plant and distribution switchboards for power reticulation.


Extensive use is made of computer databases for pricing and design. Electrical wiring diagrams are done with AutoCAD & panel designs & layouts are done using Rapsody, the Schneider Electric software for Prisma Plus type-tested enclosures.


The enclosures available are:


  • Prisma Plus System G wall-mounting & floor-standing up to 630A & 25kA
  • Prisma Plus System P floor-standing up to 3200A & 85kA

    • We are certified Prisma partners of Schneider Electric South Africa for type-tested switchboards that comply with IEC 60439-1 / SANS 10142 (RCC Number 021 1067).
