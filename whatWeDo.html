Factors to consider in the design of switchboards

  • Degree of protection against ingress of objects & water (IP rating)
  • Degree of protection against mechanical impact (IK rating)
  • Fault current rating (kA)
  • Form of seperation of the enclosure
  • Switchgear discrimination & co-ordination
  • Ease of maintenance
  • Modular design to easily upgrade

Heater Circuit Design

Powerpulse Solid State Heater Controllers (SSHC's) can control 3-phase heater banks up to 24kW.

Heater banks of larger than 24kW require auxiliary stages to be switched with contactors. Additional digital outputs will be required from the temperature control system.
